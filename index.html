From Scruffy to Clean and Everything In-Between!

Welcome to Penny's Place! In the heart of Aylmer, we provide professional grooming services designed to pamper your dogs. Since our founding in 2021, we have been dedicated to creating a safe and relaxing experience for your furry friends. At Penny's Place, we understand that grooming is not just about looks but comfort as well. Our experienced team is committed to ensuring that every visit is enjoyable for both you and your dog. We take the time to understand your dog's unique needs, providing personalized services that keep them looking and feeling their best. Our goal is to make sure you and your dog keep coming back for more!

Spa Treatment for your Dog

Our dog spa offers a luxurious experience focused on relaxation. Enjoy a warm bath with gentle shampoos, a soothing massage, and essential oils for healthy skin and fur. Don't forget to select some music for your pup!
