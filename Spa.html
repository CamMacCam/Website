Spa Treatment for your Dog

Our dog spa services offer a luxurious experience for your furry friend, focusing on relaxation and well-being. We start with a warm bath using gentle shampoos that cleanse and soothe. Following the bath, a massage can help relieve tension and finally we finish with essential oils that will keep your dogs skin and fur looking and feeling great. And don't forget to choose form a variety of music for your pup!
